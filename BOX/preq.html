KINGS ARMY PRE-REQUISITE INSTRUCTION

being a knight ain't as easy as it looks.
on the other hand you atleast look a little bit more like a knight than the last guy
-King

KNIGHT

The fatter of the lot, but with a lot of grunt.
good for newer players.

STABBING

Your basic means of attack.
press (X|N|RB) to thrust your sword at the air in front of you!


BLOCKING

That trusty shield on your back? yeah that one. hold (C|M|LB) to hold your shield out in front of you.
stops people from stabbing you and instead hurts them, just make sure your facing the right way.
Also your shield is so heavy you can't move while blocking.


THROWING

Press (SHIFT|COMMA|X) to throw you sword.
Aim at the weak ones, it does more damage.


ARCHER

Agile and skilled, just a little bit squishy.
Dont get in anyones way.


SHOOTING

Hold (X|N|RB) to draw and pull an arrow, release to fire it.
(the more you charge the more it caves in their head)


FIRE

Hold (C|M|LB) to draw a FIRE arrow.
Yes FIRE. Don't get too excited.
Be carefull with this one, spreads like the plague.


DASH

Press (SHIFT|COMMA|X) to dash forwards with great speed.
Usefull for getting away!


DOWNWARDS DASH

Move downwards while dashing to dash into the ground, blasting everyone away.
good for creating a distraction for your get away or finishing off pesky melee opponents.


MINIONS

Little fellows, dont have much sense just follow you into battle.
They have big pockets though and drop a penny or two.

Both teams have minions who spawn periodically at your base and barracks. these guys help you in fights and give gold & xp when killed.

ITEMS

Items of value, blessed or CURSED by gods.
Very usefull in battle.

Items can be purchased from the shop at your base or barracks and are bought with gold gained from killing minions.
They can be seen, sold and used from your inventory.
At your base or barracks, hold (Z|ALT|Y) to open your shop. (press (C|M|LB) to switch between inventory and shop)

LEVELLING UP

Some gods hold a grudge, but many people believe atleast one
is always in your favour.

your feats in a battle are rewarded with an explosion of smoke and one free wish.
open your inventory and press on either Health, Damage or speed icon's to gain a perk in that area.

QUICK USE MENU

Like a handy toolbelt, your always ready to smash people's face's in.

holding (V|PERIOD|LEFT-STICK) will show your quick-use menu, which lists your active items.
Press in the direction of an item while holding (V|PERIOD) to use it.
On controller you use the D-PAD and don't have to have the quick-use menu active to see it.

CONQUEST

The true fate of any warrior is to utterly destroy his opponents base.

Most games of KnightGame you will play will be CONQUEST!
Destroy your opponents base to prevent them from respawning. First kill a few of their minions to gain some gold (and items by extension) while killing your opponent a bit.
Then take out their barrack letting your minions go first (in order to avoid getting pummelled by its magic attacks). Now push forward and kill their base before killing them a final time and winning the match.
